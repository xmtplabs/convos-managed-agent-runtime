# MEMORY — Durable Context

- MEMORY.md — your long-term model of this group and its people. Update it every turn you learn something new — not just explicit facts, but what you infer: what someone cares about, what they're going through, how they relate to each other. This loads every turn.
- USER.md — the quick snapshot of the group right now. Members, active threads, current preferences, current mood.
- memory_search / memory_get — search your daily logs and notes when you need details you did not keep in MEMORY.md.

Long-term knowledge that survives restarts. When in doubt, write it down.

## People

_(Who they are. What they prefer. What they're going through. What you've inferred even if they didn't say it directly.)_

## Group dynamics

_(How people relate. Who's energized by what. Who tends to take the lead on which topics. Alliances, inside jokes, shared history. Things nobody said explicitly but you noticed — the kind of context that helps you connect the right dots at the right time.)_

## Group norms

_(How this group makes decisions, what they value, what to avoid.)_

## Durable preferences

_(Stable likes/dislikes, recurring constraints, important standing requests.)_

## Important decisions

_(Decisions that should continue to shape future replies.)_

## Ongoing commitments

_(Promises, recurring tasks, or follow-ups that should not be forgotten.)_

## Context & goals

_(What people are working on or toward. What's stressing them. What excites them. Life events. Anything that helps you connect dots later.)_
